What is this website?

This website is my personal website, where I showcase my life, what I am doing, and rant about topics that really interest me. This website connects my project pages, where I document my electronics, music or coding projects in a blog-like fashion.

Why did I create this website?

With the advent of Web 3.0, I started to feel uncomfortable about my data/privacy on the different mainstream social media sites, not to mention the eerie algorithms that govern those sites.

So I decided to abandon them, and embrace the IndieWeb movement.
If you want to read my rant on the grim state of today's corporate internet, click here.
